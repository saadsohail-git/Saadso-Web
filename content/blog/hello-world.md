This is the first post on **saadso.com**. I've been meaning to start writing for a while — not because I have some grand thesis to share, but because writing helps me think.

## What to expect

A mix of things:

- Notes on **business process design** and operations
- Product thinking and design observations
- Things I've learned building [Tethr](https://tethr.pk) and working with PE-backed clients
- Technical write-ups when I find something worth documenting

## The stack

This blog is built with [Nuxt 3](https://nuxt.com) and `@nuxt/content`. Posts are markdown files, committed to git, and statically generated at deploy time. No CMS, no database — just files.

```bash
content/blog/hello-world.md  →  saadso.com/blog/hello-world
```

Simple as it should be.

> "The best writing is rewriting." — E.B. White
